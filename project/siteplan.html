File Structure & Naming Conventions

project/ (main project folder)

├── images/ (folder for all images)

│ ├── logo.png (club logo)

│ ├── hero.jpg (hero image)

│ └── course.jpg (course image)

├── scripts/ (folder for JavaScript files)

│ └── main.js (main JavaScript file)

├── styles/ (folder for CSS files)

│ ├── base.css (base styles)

│ ├── medium.css (medium view styles)

│ └── large.css (large view styles)

├── about.html (about page)

├── contact.html (contact page)

├── index.html (home page)

└── siteplan.html (this planning document)

Naming conventions:

  • All lowercase filenames
  • Hyphens for word separation (no spaces or underscores)
  • Descriptive names that reflect content/purpose
  • Consistent folder structure following course patterns

Site Name

Arise Golf Club

This name represents a premium golf club focused on helping golfers of all levels elevate their game. The word "Arise" suggests improvement, dawn of a new day, and reaching new heights in golf performance.

Optional domain availability: arisegolfclub.com

Site Purpose

The Arise Golf Club website will serve as the primary information hub for members and prospective members, providing:

  • Information about club facilities and amenities
  • Membership options and benefits
  • Tournament schedules and results
  • Golf course conditions and tee time reservations
  • Instruction programs and pro shop information

  • What are the membership fees and what benefits do they include?

Color Schema

The color palette is inspired by golf course landscapes:

#1a3e1a
(Dark Green)
#4a7c4a
(Medium Green)
#d4af37
(Gold)
#f5f5f5
(Light Gray)

Usage:

  • Dark Green (#1a3e1a): Headers, primary buttons, important accents
  • Medium Green (#4a7c4a): Secondary elements, borders
  • Gold (#d4af37): Highlights, call-to-action elements
  • Light Gray (#f5f5f5): Background color
  • Black/Dark Gray: Body text

Typography

Font selections for the website:

  • Headings: "Montserrat", sans-serif - Clean, modern, and professional
  • Body Text: "Open Sans", sans-serif - Highly readable for content

Example:
