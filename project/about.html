About Arise Golf Club

Discover our rich history and world-class facilities

Our History

Founded in 2022, Arise Golf Club was designed by renowned architect Jude Iduh. with the vision of creating a championship course that would challenge golfers of all skill levels while preserving the natural beauty of the landscape.

For a fews years now, we've hosted numerous professional and amateur tournaments, earning a reputation as one of the premier golf destinations in the region. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the course to our clubhouse, practice facilities, and member experience.

Our Facilities

Championship Course

18 holes, par 72, 7,200 yards from the back tees with multiple tee options for all skill levels.

Practice Facilities

Driving range with 40 stations, short game area with bunkers, and putting greens replicating course conditions.

Clubhouse

20,000 square feet featuring dining, locker rooms, pro shop, and event spaces with panoramic views.
